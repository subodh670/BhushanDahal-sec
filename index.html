Questions commonly asked to junior Chef

Time

How did you get interested in cooking?

I prepared my first food when my mother taught me to cook food in home. It was only Dal bhat tarkari. Although i learned from basic i got interested from cooking videos on youtube.

How did you learn cooking in a short period of time?

I learnt cooking with many trial and errors. I learnt from myself from mistakes and created a new receipe.

Have you invented any receipe yet?

I have invented new cuisines but they were just minor to be counted. I would mix many spices and vegetables with chicken meat which eventually got me to make a new food. Ha Ha this made me invent a new food.

Have you ever been honoured for your works from co-workers?

My coworkers were so friendly that they taught me to learn new techniques with proper hospitality to guests and many things like decoration of foods as well.

Do you sometimes forget processes to make receipe that you prepared yourself before?

Forgetting receipes is a regular thing. A good practice makes a cook a good learner. So, it is a natural thing to make mistakes and forget thing until you memorize to muscle memory and be confident to cook again and again.

What do you want to say to other junior chef what to learn from you in their careers?

I only say not to stop persevering in building your career. It makes you more strong until you realize you have learnt so much and stepped up from lower position to high executive chef position.
